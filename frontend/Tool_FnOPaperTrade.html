v1 Known Limitations (Accepted)

Noted for v2 — not blocking current build

No intraday regime detection — trending vs range-bound treated equally by XGBoost
Global macro (Fed, ECB, US crash) out of scope — Indian macro only
No position sizing — all paper trades are fixed 1 lot equivalent
No correlation filter between Nifty and BankNifty trades
Weekly retrain carries survivorship bias until ~3 months of trade data accumulates
Feedback loop improves LLM context but does not auto-tune XGBoost hyperparameters
